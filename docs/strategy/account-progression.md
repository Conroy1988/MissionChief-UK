# Account Progression

## Strategy objective

Progression is not simply the number of stations owned. A healthy account expands mission generation only when fleet, staffing, transport capacity and specialist coverage can absorb the resulting demand.

## Recommended decision loop

1. Identify recurring unmet requirements.
2. Determine whether the cause is quantity, travel time, training or infrastructure.
3. Fix the narrowest bottleneck first.
4. Observe the changed mission mix.
5. Expand only when reserve capacity has recovered.

## Planned stages

- First station and initial fleet
- Early multi-station coverage
- First specialist capability
- Regional network design
- Multi-service integration
- High-volume transport management
- Advanced alliance and event operations

!!! note
    Exact fleet ratios will be presented as scenario-based templates, not universal rules.
