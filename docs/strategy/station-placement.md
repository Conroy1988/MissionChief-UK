# Station Placement and Coverage

## Core principle

MissionChief coverage is determined by operational travel and mission demand, not by aesthetically even circles on a map.

## Placement factors

- road network and bridges;
- urban congestion and routing;
- coastlines and waterways;
- hospital and custody destinations;
- alliance operating area;
- mission density;
- specialist response time;
- reserve coverage after dispatch.

## Planned tools

- placement audit checklist;
- coverage-gap worksheet;
- urban versus rural templates;
- specialist hub comparison;
- transport destination analysis;
- future map-based calculator requirements.
