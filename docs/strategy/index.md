# Strategy

Strategy pages convert verified mechanics into practical planning models.

## Planned areas

- Account progression by service and account size
- Station placement and geographic coverage
- Fleet composition and reserve capacity
- Personnel recruitment and qualification pipelines
- Classroom and training-centre planning
- Credit efficiency and expansion timing
- Dense urban versus rural operating models
- Solo play versus alliance-supported play
- Large-account dispatch and workload management
- Mobile and tablet operating workflows

Recommendations will always identify their assumptions and will not be presented as immutable game rules.
