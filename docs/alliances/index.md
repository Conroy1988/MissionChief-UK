# Alliances

The alliance section will cover both ordinary membership and large-scale administration.

## Planned coverage

- Joining and evaluating an alliance
- Roles and permissions
- Alliance funds and contribution policies
- Shared hospitals, prisons, schools and other infrastructure
- Alliance missions and events
- Large-incident coordination
- Rules, moderation and dispute handling
- Recruitment and onboarding
- Dispatch etiquette and vehicle sharing
- Operational reporting and Discord integrations

Administrative recommendations will be separated from game-enforced mechanics.
