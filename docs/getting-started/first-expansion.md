# First Expansion

This guide will define the decision gates for moving beyond the first station without creating avoidable staffing, training or vehicle deficits.

## It will cover

- when expansion is financially safe;
- whether to expand the existing service or unlock another;
- minimum reserve credits;
- personnel lead times;
- vehicle purchases versus mission demand;
- alliance support assumptions;
- urban and rural placement differences.
