# Missions and Dispatching

## Purpose

This section will document how missions generate, scale and resolve, and how responding, on-scene and still-required resources should be interpreted.

## Planned coverage

- Mission generation
- Mission requirements
- Additional and probabilistic requirements
- Responding versus on-scene resources
- Patients, prisoners and transports
- Follow-up missions
- Mission expansion and escalation
- Shared and alliance missions
- Dispatch Centre organisation
- Dispatch rules and automation tools

## Data requirement

Each mission record should eventually expose:

- canonical UK name;
- known aliases;
- mission identifier;
- service category;
- guaranteed requirements;
- probabilistic requirements;
- patient and prisoner behaviour;
- transport destinations;
- average and maximum reward where known;
- verification date and evidence source.
