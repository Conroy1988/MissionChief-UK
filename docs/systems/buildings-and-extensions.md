# Buildings and Extensions

Buildings define account capacity; extensions define specialist capability. This guide separates construction facts from progression recommendations.

## Planned coverage

- Station types
- Small and standard buildings
- Expansions and upgrade paths
- Specialist extensions
- Classrooms and training facilities
- Hospitals and departments
- Prisons and custody capacity
- Dispatch Centres
- Alliance-owned infrastructure
- Demolition, relocation and refunds

## Comparison standard

Every building entry should include cost, build time, capacity, expansion options, unlock conditions, service dependencies and verification date.
