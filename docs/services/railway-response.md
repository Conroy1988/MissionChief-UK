# Railway Police and Railway Fire Response

Railway incidents combine Police, Fire and Rescue, Ambulance, HART, Search and Rescue and specialist rail-access resources. Stage 21 establishes the first production railway-response model for MissionChief UK.

## Infrastructure

Two countable mission-generation entities are now canonical:

- `railway_police` — **Railway Police**;
- `railway_fire_response` — **Railway fire response**.

The official mission pages verify their names and generation relationships. Purchase costs, capacity and construction details remain unpublished until reproduced directly from the building interface.

## Canonical resources

Stage 21 adds:

- Road Rail Unit;
- EIU;
- Breathing Apparatus Support Unit;
- Foam Unit;
- Dog Support Unit (DSU).

EIU remains an unexpanded official abbreviation because the current primary sources do not provide a verified full name.

## Railway personnel

Current verified railway roles include:

- Railway Police Officer;
- Mobile Operations Manager;
- Police Sergeant;
- Police Inspector.

Personnel available before generation and personnel required at the incident are stored separately.

## Personnel ranges

Some railway pages provide a range rather than a single headcount, for example **14–42 firefighters**. These values use `personnel.ranges` and must not be represented as either an exact minimum or an average.

## Alternative resources

Railway missions reuse several existing independent alternative groups:

- ICCU or Ambulance Control Unit;
- HazMat Unit or CBRN Vehicle;
- Operational Support Van, Operational Support Trailer or Personal SAR Vehicle.

Each displayed row remains independent.

## POI terminology

Verified railway POIs currently include:

- Train station (regional traffic);
- Train station (regional traffic and long-distance travel);
- Goods station;
- Railroad Crossing;
- Rail Crossing;
- Subway station;
- Railway tunnel.

The guide preserves both `Railroad Crossing` and `Rail Crossing` because both labels appear in the UK game.

## Evidence boundary

The records publish only values displayed on official UK mission pages. Railway infrastructure prices, vehicle prices, staffing capacities and course durations remain separate evidence programmes.
