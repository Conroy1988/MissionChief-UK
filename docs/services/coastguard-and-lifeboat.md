# Coastguard and Lifeboat Operations

## Operational overview

Maritime response introduces launch locations, vessel classes, specialist personnel and long travel paths that behave differently from conventional road-based coverage.

## Command priorities

- Place maritime resources around navigable operational areas.
- Distinguish shore-based response from vessel response.
- Account for launch and travel constraints.
- Avoid treating different boat classes as interchangeable.
- Verify mission-specific combinations before investing heavily.

## Planned coverage

- Coastguard rescue stations
- Lifeboat stations
- Inshore and all-weather lifeboats
- Trailered rescue boats
- Coastguard vehicles
- Search and rescue support
- Personnel and training
- Maritime mission requirements
