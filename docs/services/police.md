# Police Service

## Operational overview

Police progression depends on frontline capacity, prisoner handling, specialist response, trained personnel and custody infrastructure. Poorly balanced expansion can create persistent shortages even when total vehicle numbers appear adequate.

!!! info "Evidence status"
    This page is a structured editorial base. Exact values and dependencies require current-game verification.

## Command priorities

1. Protect routine patrol capacity before deploying specialist fleets.
2. Ensure custody and prisoner transport remain proportional to demand.
3. Distribute specialist response geographically.
4. Keep qualifications attached to operational staffing plans.
5. Audit recurring mission shortages before purchasing additional stations.
