# Vehicle Catalogue

The vehicle catalogue is the central human-readable index for MissionChief UK response assets.

## Record standard

Every published vehicle record should include:

- canonical UK game name;
- known aliases and abbreviations;
- service and operational category;
- compatible building types;
- required extension, where applicable;
- minimum and maximum staffing;
- required personnel training;
- towing or carrier relationships;
- operational capabilities;
- mission dependencies;
- purchase cost and other confirmed limits;
- evidence classification and last verification date.

## Catalogue groups

### Fire and Rescue

- frontline pumping appliances;
- aerial appliances;
- command and control vehicles;
- water and foam resources;
- rescue and technical response vehicles;
- hazardous-material resources;
- wildfire resources;
- specialist carriers, towing vehicles and trailers.

### Ambulance

- emergency ambulances;
- rapid-response resources;
- patient transport vehicles;
- command resources;
- specialist clinical and hazardous-area resources;
- air ambulance resources.

### Police

- incident-response vehicles;
- traffic resources;
- armed-response resources;
- dog units;
- public-order resources;
- investigation and specialist resources;
- prisoner and custody transport.

### Maritime and rescue

- coastguard vehicles;
- lifeboats and seagoing vessels;
- inland and trailer-launched rescue boats;
- launch and towing resources;
- mountain and specialist rescue resources.

## Publication status

The catalogue architecture is active. Individual vehicle records must not be marked verified until their values have been checked against the current UK game or a reliable primary source.
