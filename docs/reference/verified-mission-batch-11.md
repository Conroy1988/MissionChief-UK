## Coastal progression

The Busy Beach and Marina records extend the existing Quiet Beach and Harbour records. Coastal complexity increases Coastguard, Bomb Disposal HQ and Marine Unit Extension requirements.

## Railway progression

The two train-station missions prove that Railway Police counts are independent Bomb Disposal generation dependencies:

- small regional station: 3 Railway Police;
- large regional and long-distance station: 6 Railway Police and one active Drone.

## Domestic and construction incidents

The Shed, Loft and Small Building Site missions establish a lower-to-medium progression path for Bomb Disposal HQ and HART dependencies.
